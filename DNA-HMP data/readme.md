Here we include the microscope data of the DNA hydrogel microparticles (DNA-HMPs) analyzed in the manuscript.

To evaluate them by the traction reconstruction methods, preprocessing has to be performed as explained in the manuscript.

The surface method analysis of one DNA-HMP from its reconstructed surface shape is presented in the notebook in the repository.

HMP_2: Reference and deformed images (c.f. Fig.S20)
